General Definition

Safe and comfortable transportation solutions in passenger elevators

Passenger elevators are systems designed primarily to transport people, where user comfort is a priority. They provide safe transportation in different building types from residential projects to hotels, hospitals and business centers.

At ASVEL Elevator, we develop solutions in line with traffic calculations and preliminary projects; in manufacturing, installation and usage stages, we take international safety and comfort standards as a basis.

Modern Passenger Elevator SystemsProject-specific, safe and aesthetic solutions for residences, hotels, hospitals and business centers.
Capacity and Use

Capacity options suitable for different building types

Systems tailored to building needs are offered with carrying capacity and number of passengers determined by area of use.

Residential Applications

In residences, systems suitable for carrying people generally between 240 kg and 1000 kg are preferred.

Commercial Buildings

In hotels, hospitals and business centers, solutions suitable for carrying people and/or goods between 630 kg and 1600 kg are applied.

Passenger Capacity

Passenger elevators can be designed with different capacity options from 3 people up to 21 people depending on project needs.

Rated Speed

Elevator speeds are planned according to building height and usage density, based on values of 0.63 m/s and above.

Technical Structure

Project-compliant technical system and manufacturing approach

Passenger elevators are planned according to building traffic and architectural design, and produced with safety, efficiency and long-lasting use in mind.

Manufacturing in Line with Design

They are manufactured and installed on site in accordance with building traffic calculations and preliminary projects.

International Standards

International safety and comfort standards are taken as the basis in production, installation and post-installation use.

Electric and Hydraulic Solutions

Electric motor-driven or hydraulic passenger elevator systems can be applied according to project requirements.
