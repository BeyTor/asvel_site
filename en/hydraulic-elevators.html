General Definition

Smooth, safe and flexible movement solutions in hydraulic elevators

Hydraulic elevators are an ideal solution for applications that require especially smooth and safe movement. Their working principle is based on converting fluid pressure into motion energy.

Hydraulic elevators stand out with performance, design flexibility and safe use advantages. Carrying capacity, building height and building function are evaluated to establish the right system configuration.

Hydraulic Elevator TechnologySmooth movement, safe use and project-specific design provide flexible solutions for different building types.
Key Advantages

Outstanding advantages of hydraulic elevators

Hydraulic elevators offer many advantages in both performance and design flexibility and can be applied efficiently according to building needs.

Machine Room Flexibility

Provides more flexible positioning options according to project needs and adapts to architectural planning.

Lower Costs

Optimizes costs by providing economical installation and operation advantages under suitable project conditions.

Fast, Smooth and Quiet Movement

Its controlled motion structure offers a comfortable, balanced and quiet user experience.

Energy Saving and Large Capacity Suitability

Can provide energy efficiency and may be preferred as an effective solution for larger capacity elevator requirements.

System Selection

Correct system selection determines efficiency in hydraulic elevators

The most suitable hydraulic elevator is selected according to carrying capacity, building height, usage purpose and the cabin movement method.

Carrying Capacity and Building Height

System design is determined according to the building height and required carrying capacity.

Direct-Acting System

One of the fundamental systems evaluated according to cabin movement method and preferred in suitable projects.

Indirect-Acting System

Another basic system used depending on hydraulic elevator type and examined according to project requirements.
