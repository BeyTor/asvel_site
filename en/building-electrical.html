General Approach

Safety and efficiency priority in building electrical projects

The safety of electrical installation, energy efficiency and detailed project drawings are the main elements that must be considered in building electrical applications.

Electrical installation safety is highly important both for building occupants and for the equipment used. Therefore, every detail should be carefully planned during the design stage and the right solutions should be preferred during implementation.

Core Elements

Main topics to consider in electrical projects

Project details should be evaluated comprehensively for a safe, efficient and long-lasting installation.

Safety Measures

Safety measures are the fundamental priority in electrical projects. The system must be designed correctly to protect both users and equipment.

Grounding and Circuit Breakers

Grounding systems and circuit breakers are among the most important parts of installation safety and must be planned correctly in the project.

Lighting and Socket Layout

Lighting and socket points should be positioned according to the project in a way that provides ease of use and functionality.

Energy Efficiency

Efficiency should be improved by using energy-efficient lighting options such as LED lamps and systems such as motion sensors.

Project Planning

Solid infrastructure with detailed drawings and proper layout

Correct evaluation of structural elements in electrical project drawings is important for a healthy implementation process.

Detailed Project Drawing

Planning all details clearly and correctly in electrical drawings prevents problems during implementation.

Planning Structural Elements

Structural elements such as walls, columns and chimneys must be correctly evaluated within the electrical project.

Application-Ready Infrastructure

Accurate planning at the design stage creates a strong foundation for both safe installation and an orderly implementation process.
